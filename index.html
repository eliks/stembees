Upcoming Events

  • Feb.13 ,2014
  • 7 - 9 p.m.
  • Hub Accra, Osu

This is a networking event and workshop for female entrepreneurs (or future entrepreneurs) in Ghana. It will take place at Hub Accra in Osu on Thursday, 13 February from 7-9PM. The event will feature Stanford MBA and management consultant Kristianne Reindorf who will speak on personal branding and creating a company culture. Amma Baffoe of MEST will speak on networking.

Next Wave Africa was founded with the intention of supporting and empowering female entrepreneurs in Africa. We are a growing network of entrepreneurs and mentors who come together on a monthly basis for workshops, networking events and competitions. Members of the group are also encouraged to speak with mentors one on one as they begin to grow their businesses.
